Herbert Flor

Herbert has experience in every design stage, from concept development, creating quick hand drawn sketches to convey idea; to GIS based analysis and thematic mapping to create engaging and informative analyses; to urban design of public realm and master planning of large project sites. He works collaboratively with a multi-disciplinary team to deliver detailed design packages.

He is highly skilled in various CADD and graphic softwares and uses these skills to create 3d models, photo-realistic rendered views, fly-through animations and even short movie clips complete with music and motion-graphic titles.

His skills also include manual and guidelines preparation, putting them together in a report format, and presenting to management and external stakeholders.
